Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

ContentBug LLC ("ContentBug", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information we collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use ContentBug:

  • Account information — name, email, billing address, and payment details you provide at signup.
  • Content — the raw video, audio, images, and briefs you upload for editing.
  • Usage data — logs of which features you use, device and browser information, and IP address.
  • Communications — messages you send to your editor or our support team.

2. How we use it

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the editing service.
  • Deliver finished edits and communicate about your projects.
  • Bill your subscription and prevent fraud.
  • Send service updates and, with your consent, marketing emails you can unsubscribe from at any time.

3. Your content

You retain full ownership of every piece of content you upload. We never sell, publish, or use your footage for training AI models or anything else outside of delivering your edits.

4. Sharing

We share data only with the service providers we need to run ContentBug — payment processing, cloud storage, and customer communication. Each is bound by a data-processing agreement. We do not sell personal information.

5. Data retention

Account data is retained while your account is active. Raw footage is retained for 90 days after a project is delivered, then permanently deleted unless you request longer retention. Billing records are retained for 7 years to meet tax obligations.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal information. Email privacy@contentbug.io and we'll respond within 30 days.

7. Security

We use industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest, role-based access controls, and logged audit trails. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable care and will notify you promptly of any breach affecting your data.

8. Children

ContentBug is not intended for anyone under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be emailed to active account holders at least 14 days before they take effect.

10. Contact

Questions? Email us at privacy@contentbug.io.
