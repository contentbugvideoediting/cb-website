1. Introduction

Content Bug Video Editing, LLC ("we," "us," or "our") describes how we gather, utilize, share, and safeguard your information when you access our website, services, and applications. By using our services, you consent to this policy's terms.

This policy applies to all Content Bug subscription plans including Basic, Silver, and Gold tiers.

2. Information We Collect

Direct Submissions

  • Account creation data (name, email, phone, address, company)
  • Payment and subscription details
  • Content for editing (videos, audio, images, creative briefs)
  • Communications with our support team
  • Meeting scheduling information
  • Employment applications

Automatic Collection

  • Device details (IP address, browser type, operating system, screen resolution)
  • Usage patterns (pages visited, features used, time spent)
  • General location based on IP address
  • System logs and access times

Third-Party Sources

  • Payment processors provide transaction data
  • Analytics providers supply usage statistics
  • Social platforms share authorized profile info
  • Cloud storage providers send file metadata

Sensitive Information

We do not intentionally collect social security numbers, financial account details, health data, or biometric identifiers. Any inadvertent submissions receive careful handling and deletion upon request.

3. How We Use Your Information

Service Delivery

We process edit requests, manage accounts, personalize experiences, and develop features based on usage analysis.

Business Operations

We process payments, send confirmations, enforce agreements, detect fraud, and maintain compliance records.

Marketing Communications

With your consent, we send promotional emails, deliver targeted advertising, conduct surveys, and feature case studies.

Legal Compliance

We respond to legal processes, government requests, and investigate policy violations as required by law.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We have not sold personal information in the past 12 months and have no intention of doing so.

Service Providers

We share data with trusted vendors who help operate our business:

  • Payment processors (Stripe)

  • Email services
  • Analytics platforms

  • Video conferencing (Zoom)
  • CRM systems (GoHighLevel)

All vendors face contractual obligations to protect your data.

Business Transfers

Information may transfer during mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales. We will provide notification in such events.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information for court orders, law enforcement requests, rights protection, policy violation investigations, and fraud prevention.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Cookie Types

  • Essential cookies: Required for functionality and security (session/persistent)
  • Functional cookies: Remember your preferences (up to 1 year)
  • Analytics cookies: Track performance and usage (up to 2 years)
  • Marketing cookies: Enable targeted advertising (up to 2 years)

Your Control

Your browser allows you to block cookies, delete sessions, or allow first-party only. Note that blocking essential cookies may affect functionality.

6. Data Retention

  • Account information: Duration of subscription plus 3 years
  • Transaction records: 7 years (legal requirement)
  • Video files: 90 days after delivery
  • Support communications: 3 years post-resolution
  • Marketing preferences: Until you opt-out
  • Analytics data: 26 months (aggregated)

Unnecessary data is securely deleted or anonymized.

7. Data Security

Our Protective Measures

  • TLS/SSL and AES-256 encryption
  • Role-based access and multi-factor authentication
  • Firewalls, intrusion detection, DDoS protection
  • 24/7 monitoring and logging
  • Third-party vendor security reviews
  • Employee security training

Your Responsibilities

  • Maintain credential confidentiality
  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Report unauthorized access immediately
  • Keep contact information current

Limitation: No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but will notify affected individuals of any breach as required by law.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Access and Portability

You may request access to your personal information and receive portable copies of your data.

Correction

You can correct inaccurate, update outdated, or complete incomplete information.

Deletion

You may request deletion of your data, subject to legal exceptions for transaction completion, legal compliance, and fraud detection.

Opt-Out Options

  • Marketing emails: Use unsubscribe links
  • SMS: Reply STOP
  • Targeted advertising: Adjust browser preferences
  • Data sharing: Contact us directly

To exercise your rights, contact contentbug@contentbug.io. We respond within 30 days after identity verification.

Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights. Service quality remains identical regardless of your choices.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents enjoy additional protections:

  • Right to Know: Request disclosure of collected categories, sources, business purposes, and third-party recipients
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of collected information (with exceptions)
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Opt-Out: We do not sell personal information or share for cross-context behavioral advertising

Authorized agents may submit requests on your behalf with proper verification.
