Questions

Frequently asked

What if I need more videos one month?
You can upgrade anytime. We prorate the switch to the higher tier for the remainder of the month.
How do revisions work?
Unlimited — you comment directly on the preview in your dashboard and your editor turns revisions around within 24 hours.
Can I pause my plan?
Yes. You can pause for up to 30 days per year at no cost. Just let your editor know.
Do unused videos roll over?
Up to 2 unused videos roll to the next month. Anything beyond that expires.
What's the longest video you'll edit?
No hard cap. Long-form pieces over 30 minutes count as 1.5 videos toward your plan limit.
